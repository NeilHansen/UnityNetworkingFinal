How To Play:

wasd or arrows to move and rotate

leftCtrl to shoot

E to pickup the flag

if you shoot the other player they will respawn, if they have the flag it will be dropped when they die

Player must pick up flag and bring it to the score zone marked in purple to get points

The yellow ball is a power up that has two effects while your holding the flag it will 
give a speed boost, without it will allow fors you to shoot faster
